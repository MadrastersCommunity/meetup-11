About the session

Design, in modern world has evolved a lot from just being a representation of plan. Similarly designers have also evolved from being a mere support function to a core business function. Engaging the current generation millennials who are less loyal when compared to previous generations which is realistic challenge for many brands today. But a few brands has decoded the formula which is DESIGN. Here is the point where designer needs to wear multiple hats by understanding the business objective, technological possibilities, their limits and resolve the problem which are core competency of any business  thus creating the need of design entrepreneurs.
People most definitely know YouTube, but who is Chad Hurley?
Chad Hurley is a smart entrepreneur with a design background; one of his notable works is the design of the original PayPal logo. After working for eBay, he decided to take his career to the next level. Having a design background he strived to solve a common problem for the end user, by focusing on contextual inquiry provided by users. Also by analyzing problems at the front stage - an experience's top-level touch points and the backstage - the processes and systems that lied beneath. Thus he founded YouTube with two of his PayPal co-workers. In 2006, YouTube was sold to Google for $1.65 billion.
